About

Hi, I'm Ale.

I build websites for local businesses — fast, clean, and made to be found on Google.

I'm Alessandro, a product manager and web developer based in Germany. I've spent years working inside agencies and tech companies, watching small businesses get ignored or overcharged.

So I started Ale Fabrik.

The idea is simple: local businesses — restaurants, artisans, shops, service providers — deserve a proper online presence. Not a rushed template from a freelance marketplace. Not a €5,000 agency project that takes six months. Something real, something that works, delivered by someone who actually cares.

I build every site myself. I write the copy. I set up your Google profile. I make sure the technical side is done properly — fast loading, mobile-friendly, compliant with GDPR.

When you work with me, you talk to me. Not a project manager, not a support ticket, not an offshore team. Me.

I'm Italian, which means I understand the local business culture in Italy and the warmth that makes it work. I'm based in Germany, which means I understand what German clients expect — thoroughness, reliability, no surprises.

I built Ale Fabrik as a side business alongside my main job, which means I'm not desperate for your money and I'm not going to oversell you. If the €299 starter site is right for your business, that's what I'll recommend.
