Questions

Things people ask before getting started.

Do I own the website?

Yes — everything is yours. The content, the design, the domain. The monthly fee covers hosting only. If you ever want to move your site elsewhere, I'll give you all the files within 10 working days, no questions asked.

What counts as a small update?

Text changes, new photos, updated hours or prices, a new menu item — anything that doesn't require rebuilding a page. Bigger changes like a new section or a new page are quoted separately, usually €50–150.

Do you work with businesses anywhere?

Yes — I work with local businesses everywhere. I write copy in Italian, German, and English, so your site speaks to your customers in their own language.

How does payment work?

Starter: you pay only when you've seen the site and you're happy to go live. Standard and Pro: 50% upfront to start the project, 50% on launch day. If you decide not to continue after work has started, we'll refund 25% of your initial payment. Monthly hosting by card, bank transfer, PayPal, or SEPA direct debit.

How is this different from using Wix or Squarespace?

With Wix or Squarespace, you build it yourself and you're locked into their platform forever. With Ale Fabrik, I build it for you, optimise it for local search from day one, and you own the result outright. No platform subscription, no learning curve, no generic template.

What happens if I stop paying for hosting?

You'll get at least 14 days written notice before anything goes offline. Your files are always yours — just ask and I'll send them over.

Not sure which package is right for you?

Tell me a bit about your business and I'll recommend the one that makes sense — honestly, even if that's the cheapest one.
