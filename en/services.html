Questions

Frequently asked.

Do I own the website?

You own everything — the content, the design, and the domain. The monthly fee is for hosting only. If you ever want to move elsewhere, I'll help you do it.

What if I need changes after launch?

Small changes (a phone number, an opening hour) are included in the care plans. Bigger changes are quoted separately — usually €50–150 depending on what's needed.

Do you work with businesses in Italy and Germany?

Yes. I'm Italian, based in Germany, and I work with businesses in both countries. I write copy in Italian, German, and English.

How do I pay?

Projects over €299: 50% upfront, 50% on launch day. Starter tier: full payment upfront. Monthly hosting by bank transfer or SEPA direct debit.

What happens if I stop paying hosting?

I'll give you at least 14 days written notice, then the site goes offline. Your files are always available to you — just ask and I'll send them over.

Not sure which package?

Drop me a message and I'll tell you what makes sense for your business. No obligation.
